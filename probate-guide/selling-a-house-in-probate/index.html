Selling A House In Probate

What executors should know before selling a probate property.

Probate can be stressful, particularly when a property is involved. Executors often need to balance legal duties, family expectations, property security, contents, insurance and sale timelines.

What this means for selling

A direct probate property sale can provide certainty where the property is empty, dated, difficult to mortgage or where beneficiaries prefer a clear outcome without repeated viewings.
