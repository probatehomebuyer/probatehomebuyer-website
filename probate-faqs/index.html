Probate Property Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about probate, inherited properties and executor sales.

Can you sell a house before probate is granted?

A property can often be marketed before probate is granted, but completion usually cannot take place until the Grant of Probate has been issued.

What happens to a mortgage during probate?

Mortgage payments normally continue during probate. Executors are responsible for managing the estate assets and liabilities until the estate is distributed.

Do all beneficiaries need to agree to sell?

This depends on the will structure and legal ownership. In most cases, executors manage the sale process on behalf of the estate.

Can you sell an inherited property quickly?

Yes. Specialist probate buyers can often purchase properties much faster than traditional open-market buyers.

What if the inherited property needs refurbishment?

Many inherited properties require updating or repair work. Probate Home Buyer purchases properties in any condition.
