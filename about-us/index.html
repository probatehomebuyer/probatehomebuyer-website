About Probate Home Buyer

Trusted probate property buyers helping families, executors and beneficiaries sell inherited homes quickly, discreetly and with certainty.

Request a free cash offer

Specialists in probate and inherited property sales

Probate Home Buyer was created to give families a simpler alternative when dealing with an inherited property. We understand that probate sales are rarely straightforward. Properties can be empty, dated, tenanted, difficult to manage, or tied up with paperwork while executors and beneficiaries decide the best way forward.

Our role is to make the property side of probate easier. We provide clear advice, direct cash offers and flexible completion dates, helping estates avoid long chains, repeated viewings and the uncertainty of a traditional sale.

A professional and compassionate approach

We know that selling a property during probate often comes at a difficult time. Families may be dealing with bereavement, legal documents, inheritance tax, household contents, insurance, security and ongoing bills.

Every enquiry is handled with discretion and respect. We take time to understand the situation and provide a practical route forward, whether you need a quick sale or a completion date that works around the Grant of Probate.
