About Probate Home Buyer

A straightforward way to sell an inherited property

Probate Home Buyer helps families, executors and beneficiaries sell inherited and probate properties across England.

We understand that dealing with probate can be stressful, especially where a property is empty, dated, difficult to manage or needs to be sold within a specific timescale.

Our aim is simple: provide a professional, discreet and reliable alternative to a traditional estate agent sale.

Why families choose us

  • Fast and flexible cash purchases
  • No estate agent fees or hidden costs
  • No chains or mortgage delays
  • We buy properties in any condition
  • Discreet and confidential service
  • Experienced with probate property sales

Experienced property buyers

Our team has extensive experience reviewing and purchasing residential property across England, including inherited homes, vacant properties and properties requiring refurbishment.

We work flexibly around executors, beneficiaries and solicitors, whether probate has already been granted or the estate is still going through the legal process.

2500+ properties reviewed

Practical experience across a wide range of property types and conditions.

Direct buyer

We are not an estate agent and do not charge estate agency commission.

Flexible completion

We can work around probate timings and solicitor requirements.
