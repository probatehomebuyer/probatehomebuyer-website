Can You Sell A Probate Property Before Probate Is Granted?

In many cases, a probate property can be prepared for sale and terms can be agreed before the Grant of Probate is issued. Completion usually takes place once the legal authority to sell is in place.

This can help executors reduce delays because a buyer is ready once probate is granted.
