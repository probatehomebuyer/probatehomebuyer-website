This Cookie Policy explains how Probate Home Buyer, operated by Incor Property Ltd, uses cookies and similar technologies on this website.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites work properly, remember preferences, understand visitor behaviour and measure advertising performance.

2. Cookies we may use

We may use essential cookies needed for the website to function, analytics cookies to understand how visitors use the website, and advertising or conversion cookies to measure Google Ads performance and improve future campaigns.

We use Google Analytics and Google Ads tools, including conversion tracking, to understand traffic, measure submitted enquiries and assess advertising performance. Google may use cookies or similar technologies to collect information about your visit, device and browser.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect how the website works or how accurately we can measure advertising performance.

5. Cookie banner

Where shown, our cookie banner allows you to acknowledge the use of cookies. We may update the banner or cookie controls as our website and advertising setup develops.
